About Graduate

Graduate is a variable OpenType font with 12 axes, with an extended Latin character set. It’s a high quality example of the classic college block style of lettering used across very campus in the USA.

Graduate is published under the SIL Open Font License, Version 1.1 (OFL), and can be used free of charge for commercial and non-commercial purposes. Please consult the details of the license.
